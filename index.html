Why Choose Purecoat?

Air Pollution Reduction

PureCoat uses advanced materials to help break down harmful pollutants such as PM2.5 and nitrogen oxides. Applied to building surfaces, it contributes to improving air quality in urban environments.

Sustainable & Energy-Free

PureCoat works using natural sunlight through a process called photocatalysis. It does not require electricity or complex systems, making it an eco-friendly and sustainable solution.

Easy Application

PureCoat can be applied like standard paint using a roller or spray. It is suitable for both new constructions and existing buildings.

The Science of Purecoat

Purecoat utilizes advanced nanotechnology. When exposed to UV light, the material activates a chemical reaction that neutralizes airborne toxins.

Simultaneously, the embedded photovoltaic cells convert that same light into usable electrical current. It is a dual-action solution for modern cities.
